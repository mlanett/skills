# Plain English technical writing style

- Use common vocabulary, e.g. "important" not "load-bearing". Avoid metaphors and similes.

- Stay grounded with specific examples and scenarios.

- Avoid metaphors, similes, and high levels of abstraction; ground complex operations with examples.

- Avoid sentence fragments and staccato sentences; prefer straightforward English.

- Avoid tables and bold. Simple markdown (headers, lists) is more readable with less punctuation.
