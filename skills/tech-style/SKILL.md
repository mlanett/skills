# Technical Writing Style Guide

> Based on Strunk & White; Revised for technical writing.

Choose a suitable design and hold to it.

Do not explain too much.

In summaries, keep to one tense.

Make assertions positive.

Make the paragraph the unit of composition.

Omit needless words; make every word tell.

Place yourself in the background.

Prefer the standard to the offbeat.

Revise and rewrite.

Use definite, specific, concrete language; avoid vague abstractions in favor of clear, definite language.

Use the active voice; active voice makes writing more vigorous and direct than passive voice.
